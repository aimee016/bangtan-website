Name:Kim Seokjin

Stage Name: Jin

Birthday: 12/04/1992

Position: Vocalist, Visuals

INFORMATION

  • Jin originally wanted to become an actor
  • Although introverted, he has many friends within the K-pop industry
  • He was scouted on the bus because of his good looks
  • His voice was described as tender, sorrowful, and free spirited
  • He started formally singing at the age of 20
  • He co-produced the song "Awake" in 2016 and released a Christmas version later on that year
  • He released the song "Tonight" as his first independent song for a yearly event celebrating the anniversary of their debut
  • He released the song "Abyss" on December 3rd 2020, emulating his feelings of depression and burnout.
  • He released a song called "Super Tuna" with a special choreography video on his birthday in 2021. As of February 1, 2022, it has reached 50 million streams on Spotify.
