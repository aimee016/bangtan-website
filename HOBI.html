Name: Jung Hoseok

Stage Name: J-Hope

Birthday: 02/18/1994

Position: Main Dancer, Producer, Lead Rapper

INFORMATION

  • His music is described as upbeat and energetic
  • His nickname "Hobi" was derived from his stage name J-Hope.
  • He has an older sister and is very private about his personal life
  • He released Hope World in 2018.
  • He was promoted to a full-time member of Korean Music Copyright Association (KOMCA) in January 2020
  • He released a single called "Chicken Noodle Soup" featuring Becky G.
  • He released a full 3 minute version of his song "Blue Side (Outro)" on his mixtape "Hope World"s 3-year anniversary on March 1, 2021
