Name:Min Yoongi

Stage Name: Suga, Agust D

Birthday: 03/09/1993

Position: Producer, Lead Rapper

INFORMATION

  • Originally auditioned to become a producer but eventually became an idol
  • He has recorded with artists such as Suran, IU, Lee So-ra, Halsey, Juice WRLD, and ØMI.
  • was promoted to a full-time member of Korean Music Copyright Association (KOMCA) in 2018
  • His lyrics often involve themes that "are full of dreams and hope".
  • He released his mixtape called AGUST D in August 2016
  • His song Daechwita has the same beat throughout, his rapping skills giving the illusion that the beat changes
  • He had his appendicitis treated in December 2013, rendering him unable to join year-end activities
  • He had an ear injury in December 2016, and was advised not to do choreography for a week, which left him out of year-end activties once again
  • He had surgery in November 2020 for his torn shoulder, and announced he was taking a break to fully recover.
  • He has contracted COVID-19 in December 2021 and was asymptomatic. He had recovered in January 2022
