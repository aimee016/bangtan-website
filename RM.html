Name:Kim Namjoon

Stage Name: RM (previously Rap Monster)

Birthday: 09/12/1994

Position:Leader, Producer, Main Rapper

INFORMATION

  • RM formally changed his stage name from Rap Monster in 2017
  • He has recorded with artists such as Wale, Younha, Warren G, Gaeko, Krizz Kaliko, MFBTY, Fall Out Boy, Primary, Steve Aoki, and Lil Nas X.
  • He was the first member recruited by their company BigHit
  • His lyrics often feature clever wordplay and beautiful imagery
  • He has over 180 songs credited under his name in Korea Music Copyright Association (KOMCA)
  • As of 2020, he has been promoted to a full-time member in KOMCA
  • He is also the youngest and 2nd most credited artist under the association
